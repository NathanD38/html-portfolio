The Best Movies According to Nathan

My top 3 movies of all time.

O, Brother, Wher Art Thou?

Hilarious movie about a group of convicts becoming nation-wide bluegrass sensation.

Donnie Darko

An amazing Sci-Fi movie that will surely mess with your mind.

The Prestige

The twist at the end always gets me. A rivalry between two illusionists at the turn of the 20th century.
